Who We Are

About InkShift

We create professional AI-generated cover art tailored for webnovel authors. Using cutting-edge tools across multiple platforms, we deliver genre-specific covers that capture the essence of your story.

⚔

Genre Expertise

We specialise in fantasy, romance, and progression fantasy — the core genres of the webnovel ecosystem. Every cover is crafted with deep understanding of reader expectations for each niche.

⚙

Multi-Tool Pipeline

We use ChatGPT/DALL-E 3, RunwayML, and Stable Diffusion — selecting the right tool for each genre. Soft digital painting for romance, bold comic art for action, detailed fantasy for LitRPG.

★

Author-First Pricing

Professional covers at a fraction of traditional illustration costs. Fast turnaround, iterative revisions, and pricing designed for self-publishing authors — not corporate budgets.
