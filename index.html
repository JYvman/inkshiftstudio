Artist Statement

InkShift Studio exists because webnovel authors deserve covers that match the quality of their stories without breaking the bank. The webnovel ecosystem is huge and growing, but the gap between what readers expect visually and what most authors can afford is massive.

I use AI as a tool, not a replacement for creative decision-making. Every cover involves deliberate choices about genre conventions, composition, mood, and colour palette. The AI generates, but the human directs. Choosing which model to use, how to prompt it, which outputs to keep, and how to iterate on them is where the actual creative work happens.

The covers in this portfolio aren't generic AI outputs. They're genre-specific compositions designed for specific subgenres within the webnovel space, built by someone who actually reads and understands these stories.
